# Incident Response

> _Placeholder — adapt to your on-call setup._

## Severity levels

| Level | Meaning | Response |
|-------|---------|----------|
| SEV1  | Major outage | Page on-call immediately |
| SEV2  | Partial degradation | Respond during business hours |
| SEV3  | Minor / cosmetic | Track as a normal issue |

## When an incident starts

1. Declare the incident and assign an incident commander.
2. Open a dedicated comms channel.
3. Mitigate first, diagnose second.
4. After resolution, write a blameless postmortem.
